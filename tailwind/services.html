CATEGORY

ALLERGIES

If someone is suffering from severe allergy or anaphylaxis call medical emergency helpline immediately.
While waiting for ambulance you can follow these first aid steps.

  1. 1.Try to identify the allergen and the person further contact with it.
  2. 2.Allow the Patient to rest especially during breathing difficulties and don't give the person anything by mouth to drink or eat.
  3. 3.Check the persons breathing, pulse and level of consciousness.

CATEGORY

HEART ATTACK

If someone had heart attack.

  1. 1.Raise the victims shoulders, so that he is half siting and support him with cushion or pillow.
  2. 2.Reassume him and keep him calm as much as possible.
  3. 3.Loosen the tight clothing, which is restricting the victims breathing like shirt, tie, belt, etc.
  4. 4.If victim loses unconsciousness at any moment, open his airway and be prepared to perform CPR.

CATEGORY

BURNS

First aid for burns is decided based on degree of burns. If series burn ,call medical Ememrgency helpline.

  1. 1.Protect the victim from further harm. If the victim clothing is on fire, place her in ground and use cotton or wool blanket to smother the flames.
  2. 2.Cool, the burn under the cool running water for atleast 10 to 15 minutes, until the pain eases.
  3. 3.Monitor the victims breathing, pulse and levelof consciousness for every 10 min and check out any signs for shock.
